what is a box model?

A box model is a design layout that wraps all HTML elements,

main properties of a box model.

  1. Margin-This defines where a line of text begins and ends.
  2. Border-This is what goes around the padding and content.
  3. Content-THis is where texts and images appear.
  4. Padding-Thisnois the space between its content and its Border.
